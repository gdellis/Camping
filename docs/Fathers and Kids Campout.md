## Food
### 🍔Dinner
No dinner will be provided.  You can eat before or bring something with you.  You are welcome to bring tin foil dinners and cook them on the fire.

### 🥞 Breakfast 
Breakfast will be provided.
- Pancakes
- Eggs/Cheese
- Bacon
- Milk (Chocolate / White)
- Muffins
